Supermarket Sales Analysis Using Tableau Project Overview

This project analyzes supermarket sales data using Tableau dashboards and visualizations. The dataset contains historical sales records collected from three different branches over a period of three months. The main objective is to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends through interactive visual analytics.

Dataset Description

The dataset represents historical supermarket sales across three branches. It includes information such as product lines, payment methods, customer ratings, gender, quantity sold, gross income, and sales dates. The data helps evaluate market competition and customer purchasing patterns.

DA ASSIGNMENT -1

1. Bar Chart – Total Sales by Product Line

Analyzes which product category generates the highest revenue.

2. Pie Chart – Payment Method Distribution

Shows customer preference among different payment modes.

3. Stacked Bar Chart – Product Line by Gender

Displays gender-wise purchasing behavior across product lines.

4. Line Chart – Sales Over Time

Tracks overall sales performance across the three-month period.

5. Bubble Chart – Rating vs Gross Income

Explores the relationship between customer satisfaction and income generated.

Assignment – 2 Visualizations

1. Donut Chart – Total Sales by Product Line

Represents the proportion of sales contributed by each product category.

2. Area Chart – Sales Trend Over Time by Branch

Shows branch-wise sales trends over the given time period.

3. Text Table – Quantity Sold by Product Line and Gender

Displays number of items sold segmented by gender.

4. Highlighted Table – Gross Income by Branch and Payment Type

Uses color coding to compare gross income across branches and payment methods.

5. Word Cloud – Most Frequent Product Lines Purchased

Highlights commonly purchased product lines based on quantity.

6. Funnel Chart – Quantity Sold per Product Line

Shows product performance and sales drop-off across categories.

7. Waterfall Chart – Gross Income Breakdown by Product Line

Illustrates cumulative income contribution of each product line.
